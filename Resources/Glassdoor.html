    Position Overview

    Responsible for assisting the Data Intelligence team to address reporting and analysis requests, gathering requirements for reporting tools, validating data results and publication of routine reports from the Data Intelligence team. Provides support for data intelligence, ad-hoc analytics and facilitating report design implementation with IT development teams.

    KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
    • Assist with analysis and reporting tasks including; gathering and documenting report requirements, reconciliation and QA of results.
    • Navigate and extract data to provide custom reporting and analysis on demand. Establish dashboard prototypes for development.
    • Monitor and report on trends in KPI and outlier data.
    • Work closely with development team to coordinate business requirements and output of reports.
    • Maintain standard reporting publications on a monthly and quarterly basis.
    • Report ongoing results to product owners and identify positive or negative trends in KPI.
    • Coordinate execution of ad-hoc report and analysis requests.
    • Use all available data to monitor operational KPI and raise potential outliers or risks to KPI goals.
    • Work closely with Data Intelligence teams to turn data driven findings into operational successes.
    • Maintain awareness of and ensure adherence to Zelis standards regarding privacy and professionalism.
    • Demonstrated proficiency in crafting meaningful analytics to help drive operational efficiencies.
    • Demonstrated proficiency in bridging the gap between technical and non-technical teams to produce valuable tools and information.
    • Working knowledge of relational database concepts and SQL server
    • Advanced knowledge of Microsoft Excel or similar tools
    • High attention to detail and demonstrated proficiency in reconciliation.
    • Experience with Azure HDFS technology a plus
    • Experience developing dashboard tools in Power BI a plus
    PROFESSIONAL EXPERIENCE/QUALIFICATIONS
    • 2+ years business intelligence or reporting responsibility in operational or financial reporting environments.
    • 2+ years in business analyst role or similar cross functional role working between technical and non-technical teams.
    • Ability to accurately document and communicate reporting requirements.
    • Able to evaluate data and reports for trends, and significant impact drivers.
    • Advanced knowledge of Microsoft Excel or similar tools.
    • Experience with SQL and relational databases a plus.
    • Healthcare data experience a plus.
    COMPENSATION:

    An attractive compensation package as well as comprehensive benefits plans are available to attract outstanding candidates.
